Multiplication of matrices

---



$\begin{bmatrix}
3 & 1 & 2 & 1 \\
2 & 2 & 4 & 1 \\
\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}
5\\
2\\
3\\
3\\
\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}
26\\
29
\end{bmatrix}$


---

Multiplying a matrix by the identity matrix has no effect.

$\begin{bmatrix}
3 & 2 \\
-1 & 2 \\
\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 \\
0 & 1 \\
\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}
3 & 2 \\
-1 & 2 \\
\end{bmatrix}$
