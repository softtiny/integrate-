### Plain

```math
\begin{matrix}
1 & 2 & 3\\
a & b & c
\end{matrix}
```

### Parentheses; round brackets 

```math
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3\\
a & b & c
\end{pmatrix}
```

### Brackets; square brackets

```math
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3\\
a & b & c
\end{bmatrix}
```

### Braces; curly brackets

```math
\begin{Bmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3\\
a & b & c
\end{Bmatrix}
```

### Pipes

```math
\begin{vmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3\\
a & b & c
\end{vmatrix}
```

### Double pipes 

```math
\begin{Vmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3\\
a & b & c
\end{Vmatrix}
```
